# Weekly Team Reporting Format

This reference defines the preferred short form for weekly team operations notes.

## Weekly Team Plan

Recommended sections:

1. Summary
2. This Week Focus
3. Owners
4. Blockers
5. Dependencies
6. Risks
7. Leadership Note

## Weekly Team Review

Recommended sections:

1. Summary
2. What Moved Forward
3. What Did Not Move
4. Carry-Over
5. Blockers
6. Dependency Notes
7. Team Health Signal
8. Next Week Attention

## Org Weekly Summary

Recommended sections:

1. Executive Summary
2. Team-by-Team Snapshot
3. Major Blockers
4. Cross-Team Dependencies
5. Leadership Attention Needed
6. Next Week Preview

## Style Rules

- Keep every section short
- Prefer bullets over long narrative
- Show owners where relevant
- Distinguish blockers from risks
- Mark assumptions clearly
- Flag stale weekly notes explicitly
